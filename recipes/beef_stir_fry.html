Recipe Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Season the ground beef and brown it in olive oil until cooked through.
  2. Drain grease and set aside.
  3. Deglaze the skillet with white wine (or broth), and let it reduce by half.
  4. Add the broccoli, green beans, mushrooms, onions, and celery and cook for 3 minutes, or until slightly softened.
  5. Add the carrots and bell peppers and cook for 2 more minutes.
  6. Add the sauce mixture and bring to a gentle bubble, reduce for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add cornstarch slurry to the bubbling sauce and stir to combine.
  8. Reduce heat to low and add the beef back.
  9. Stir to combine and allow it to heat through.
  10. Top with desired garnishes and serve with rice or noodles.
